Phishing

What is it, and how can I avoid it?

What is Phishing?

Phishing is the act of attempting to acquire sensitive information such as usernames, passwords, and credit card details by masquerading as a trustworthy entity in an electronic communication.

Example of a phishing message I was sent on steam

  • Here you can see that the username is trying to be
    "PLAYERUNKNOWNS BATTLEGROUNDS"
    But instead it's
    "PLAYEROUNKNOMS BATTLEGROUND"
    Named after a popular game.
  • Pretty generic scam saying
    "Hey! You won a prize! Click this link to redeem it!"
  • You can also see the link starts with 31101G.asia which seems very suspicious.
  • You will also notice that it ends with pubg.com so that at a quick glance, it looks legit.

How can I avoid it?

Here are some tips to avoid phishing

  • Never click on links in emails or messages
  • Never give out personal information
  • Never give out your password
  • Never give out your credit card information
  • Never give out your social security number

What to do if you think you have been phished

Here are some tips on what you can do

  • Change your password
  • Deactivate compromised credit cards
  • Change your social security number
